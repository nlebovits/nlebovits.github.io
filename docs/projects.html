Gun Crimes Dashboard

Based on the work of Dr. Eugenia South, I have been building a dashboard to help community organizations prioritize vacant and abandoned parcels for anti-gun violence greening interventions. I am currently working to develop a more sustainable, accessible, and reproducible version of the dashboard with a combination of Python and JavaScript. I hope to launch a public version in the fall of 2023. In the meantime, you can see an ArcGIS draft of the dashboard that I made in November of 2022.
