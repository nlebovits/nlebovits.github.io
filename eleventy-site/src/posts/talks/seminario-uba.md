A workshop and seminar on open data for sustainable cities, delivered at Universidad de Buenos Aires. The session covered exploring open data portals, real-world applications of open data in urban planning, and a hands-on workshop using Google Colab for spatial data analysis.

**Topics covered:**
- Open data portals (Buenos Aires, federal government, Google Earth Engine)
- Case studies of open data applications
- Hands-on spatial data analysis workshop
